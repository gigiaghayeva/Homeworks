Home Made Apple Pie Recipe

Ingredients:

  • pie crust
  • apples
  • granulated sugar
  • brown sugar
  • flour
  • cinnamon
  • nutmeg
  • lemon
  • lemon
  • egg

    • Instructions

    1. Bake the pie at 400°F for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove the pie shield
    3. lower the oven temperature to 375°F
    4. bake an additional 30-35 minutes.

    Serving Suggestion

    Serve your homemade apple pie warm with a scoop of vanilla

    ENJOY!
